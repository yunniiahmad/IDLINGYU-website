About

A structured, warm approach to learning English.

IDLINGYU was built around one idea: English lessons should feel structured enough to show real progress, and warm enough that learners actually want to keep coming back. Every level is planned unit by unit, with clear speaking, writing, and confidence goals — not improvised week to week.

Lessons are led by Yuni, who designs and teaches every unit personally, from lesson plans and slide decks to worksheets, parent updates, and final assessments.

Fully mapped curriculum

Every level is broken into units with a lesson plan, slide outline, worksheet, and assessment — so nothing is taught on the fly.

Progress you can see

Regular checkpoints and a final review project give parents and adult learners clear evidence of what's been learned.

Built for real communication

Every unit works toward a speaking or writing output, not just vocabulary lists — the goal is confident, usable English.

Curriculum

Two tracks, fully mapped from beginner to confident speaker.

Each level is organized into themed units — a lesson plan, slide deck outline, worksheet, parent note, and assessment checklist for every single one.

Why it's built this way — this curriculum comes from lived experience, not theory alone. It grew out of my own path through anxiety and grief toward confident daily English practice, and later, teaching children in a Montessori-based classroom — where I learned that emotional safety, consistency, and real-world practice build fluency faster than pressure ever could. Every level below follows those same principles, mapped to CEFR outcomes at each stage.
EFK · Ages 5–7

Level I — Foundation Communicator

Pre-A1 to A1. Builds first words, classroom routines, and speaking confidence.

  • Unit R — Readiness Phase
  • Hello & Me, Numbers & Colours
  • Classroom Language, Family, Objects
  • Feelings, Animals
  • Unit 8 — Review Project
EFK · Ages 7–9

Level II — Emerging Speaker

A1. Expands into personal information, routines, and everyday topics.

  • Personal Info, Daily Routine
  • Food, People & Appearance
  • Places, Abilities
  • Time
  • Unit 8 — Review Project
EFK · Ages 9–12

Level III — Independent Communicator

A1+ to A2 bridge. Moves into storytelling, planning, and independent tasks.

  • Past, Future, Directions
  • Shopping, Health
  • Social language, Stories
  • Unit 8 — Final Review Project
General English (GE) runs alongside EFK for teens and adults, from A1 (survival and basic personal communication) through A2 (everyday interaction and simple opinions) toward B1 and B2 — each level built as a full unit-by-unit programme with speaking and writing outputs, homework, and assessment checklists.
